# PRIVITY - Share Booking System
## Official Launch Announcement

---

### Introducing PRIVITY: Your Complete Private Equity Management Platform

We are excited to announce the official launch of **PRIVITY**, a comprehensive Share Booking System designed specifically for our organization's private equity operations. This modern, secure, and intuitive platform will streamline our share booking processes, inventory management, and financial reporting.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Client Management
- **Complete Client Lifecycle**: Create, manage, and track client information from onboarding to transaction completion
- **Multi-Type Support**: Handle Proprietors, Partnerships, Companies, HUFs, Trusts, and more
- **Document Management**: Upload and manage KYC documents, agreements, and compliance files
- **Portfolio View**: See each client's complete transaction history and holdings
- **Bulk Upload**: Import multiple clients via Excel spreadsheet

### 2. Stock & Inventory Management
- **Real-Time Inventory Tracking**: Monitor available stock quantities across all holdings
- **Weighted Average Price (WAP)**: Automatic calculation of actual cost price
- **Landing Price (LP)**: Set customer-facing prices with PE Desk control
- **Blocked Quantity Tracking**: View stocks reserved for pending bookings

### 3. Booking System
- **Streamlined Booking Creation**: Create bookings with automatic price population
- **Client Confirmation Workflow**: Automated email/WhatsApp confirmation to clients
- **Multi-Level Approval**: PE Desk approval for all bookings with loss-booking escalation
- **DP Transfer Tracking**: Complete tracking from booking to share transfer
- **Revenue Calculations**: Automatic profit/loss and commission calculations

### 4. Financial Management
- **Payment Tracking**: Record and track client payments and refunds
- **Commission Management**: Automatic calculation of employee and partner commissions
- **Referral Partner Payments**: Track RP revenue shares and payment status
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Handle INR transactions with proper formatting

### 5. Reporting & Analytics
- **P&L Reports**: Comprehensive profit and loss analysis with export options
- **PE Desk HIT Report**: Track margin captured between LP and WAP
- **Revenue Dashboards**: View team performance, RP revenue, and employee earnings
- **Audit Trail**: Complete logging of all system activities
- **Export Options**: Download reports in Excel and PDF formats

### 6. Research & Insights
- **Research Reports Library**: Centralized storage for stock research reports
- **AI-Powered Assistant (Sohini)**: Ask questions about stocks and get AI-generated insights
- **Document Search**: Find relevant research across your library

### 7. Security & Compliance
- **Role-Based Access Control**: 9 different user roles with granular permissions
- **Security Dashboard**: Monitor login activity, suspicious attempts, and geographic access
- **Session Management**: Track active sessions with ability to force logout
- **IP Blocking**: Automatic blocking after failed login attempts
- **Azure AD Integration**: Optional SSO with Microsoft accounts

### 8. Communication Tools
- **Email Integration**: Automated emails for confirmations, approvals, and notifications
- **Real-Time Notifications**: Instant alerts for important events via WebSocket
- **Contract Notes**: Generate and send professional contract notes to clients
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Send booking confirmations via WhatsApp
